The CSV file should start with a line of headings, identifying the columns following. The order of the headings doesn't matter, nor does the case or any whitespace or non-alphanumeric characters (which are stripped out), but the words should be as listed in bold below. The content of the column in question is also as described.

  • taxon (the name of the taxon)
  • gr (an OS grid reference or a tetrad)
  • recorder (one or more names of people or entities, separated by semi-colons)
  • year (a positive integer)
  • month (a positive integer from 1 to 12; optional)
  • day (a positive integer from 1 to 31; optional)

Extra headings are allowed, these will be ignored.
