About this project

The Mosses and Liverworts of Cheshire is a project that aims to document the distribution of all bryophyte species in vice-county 58. Beginning in 2013, the goal is to complete surveying by the end of 2018. The maps illustrated here show progress to-date. For further information, please contact Des Callaghan (des.callaghan@ecostudy.co.uk).
